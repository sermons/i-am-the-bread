  + crowds followed
+ "I am": who do **people** say He is?
  + teacher/rabbi, prophet/messiah,
  + rebel/king of ascendant military power

  + walking on water, to discip:
  + "It is I; fear not"

  + give me enough patience for today
  + enough grace toward the unlovables 
  + enough humility under God's authority
